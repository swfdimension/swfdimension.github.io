Why no Unity games are on SWFWorld

Susorodni


A lot of requests for games in the feedback page are for Unity games, such as Rooftop Snipers, Ping Pong Chaos, and more. Unity shut down their Unity Web Player about a year ago, and since then it has been really hard to add Unity Games to websites, making it extremely difficult for us to add Unity games. We are still trying to figure out how to add them, so we will tell you once we are able to.
